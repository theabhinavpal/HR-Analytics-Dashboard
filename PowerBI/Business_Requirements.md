# Business Requirements — HR Analytics Dashboard

## Stakeholder
VP of People / Chief HR Officer, supported by department heads and Talent Acquisition.

## Problem Statement
The company has grown to 5,400+ employees across 8 departments and 4 regions. HR leadership lacks a single, self-service view of workforce composition, attrition drivers, performance, and compensation — decisions are currently made on anecdote rather than data. Attrition has been rising in specific pockets (Sales, younger employees, overtime workers) but no one can quantify or explain it without pulling ad-hoc exports.

## Goals
1. Give executives a single-page pulse check (headcount, attrition rate, salary, satisfaction) refreshed on demand.
2. Let HR business partners drill into *why* people are leaving, by department, role, age band, and satisfaction driver.
3. Give managers visibility into performance, training investment, and promotion equity.
4. Give Compensation & Benefits a defensible view of pay bands and pay-attrition relationship.

## In Scope
- Historical employee data 2016–2026 (hires and exits)
- Departmental, regional, and role-level breakdowns
- Attrition, performance, satisfaction, compensation, and demographic analysis

## Out of Scope
- Real-time payroll integration
- Predictive ML attrition scoring (flagged as a bonus/future enhancement)
- Individual performance review workflow

## 50 Business Questions the Dashboard Must Answer

**Attrition**
1. What is the overall attrition rate?
2. Which department has the highest attrition rate?
3. Which job role has the highest attrition rate?
4. Which age group leaves most frequently?
5. Does overtime correlate with attrition?
6. Does salary level correlate with attrition?
7. Does work-life balance correlate with attrition?
8. What are the top reasons employees give for leaving?
9. Which business unit/region has the highest attrition?
10. What % of total attrition is concentrated in the top 5 job roles?
11. Is there a monthly seasonality pattern to resignations?
12. Does promotion history affect the likelihood of leaving?
13. Does commute distance affect attrition?
14. Is attrition higher for remote, hybrid, or onsite employees?
15. Does gender affect attrition rate?

**Demographics**
16. What is the gender distribution across the company?
17. What is the age distribution of the workforce?
18. What is the marital status breakdown?
19. Which education level is most common?
20. Which education field has the highest average performance?
21. How is headcount distributed by department?
22. How is headcount distributed by location/country?
23. What is the average age by department?
24. What % of the workforce is on contract vs. full-time?
25. What is the job-level distribution (seniority pyramid)?

**Compensation**
26. Which department has the highest average salary?
27. Which job role has the highest average salary?
28. What is the average bonus as a % of salary?
29. How does salary vary by job level?
30. What is the pay gap (if any) between genders?
31. What is the salary distribution by quartile, and how does attrition vary across quartiles?
32. What is the average monthly income by department?

**Tenure & Growth**
33. What is the average employee tenure?
34. What is the average years of experience?
35. How many promotions occurred in the last 12 months?
36. What is the promotion rate by department?
37. What is the monthly hiring trend, and is there seasonality?
38. What is the monthly resignation trend?
39. What is net headcount growth month over month?

**Performance & Development**
40. What is the performance rating distribution?
41. What is the average training hours by department?
42. Does training investment correlate with performance rating?
43. Which departments/roles have the top performers?
44. What is the relationship between performance rating and promotion?

**Satisfaction & Wellbeing**
45. What is average job satisfaction, environment satisfaction, and work-life balance?
46. How does job satisfaction relate to absenteeism?
47. Which department has the highest satisfaction scores?
48. What % of employees report poor work-life balance (1–2 out of 4)?
49. What is the average absenteeism, and which segments drive it?
50. What is the average remaining leave balance across the company?

**Operational**
51. What % of employees work remote / hybrid / onsite?
52. How does travel frequency vary by department?
